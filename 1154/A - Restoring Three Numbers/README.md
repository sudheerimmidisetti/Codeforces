So, there are four numbers on a board in random order: $$$a+b$$$, $$$a+c$$$, $$$b+c$$$ and $$$a+b+c$$$.</p><p>You have to guess three numbers $$$a$$$, $$$b$$$ and $$$c$$$ using given numbers.
